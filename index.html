A National Icon is Born

Since 1923, Vegemite has occupied a proud place in Australian households, symbolising more than just a spread, it embodies our national identity.

Essential Nutrition.

Crafted from premium brewers’ yeast extract and enriched with essential B vitamins (B1, B2, B3, and folate), it delivers a distinctive, savory flavor while supporting energy metabolism, cellular health, and overall wellbeing. With no added sugar and a commitment to being 100% vegetarian, Vegemite is a trusted source of nutrition.

A Taste of Tradition.

From the classic school lunch to modern cafés, Vegemite has evolved gracefully through the generations while continuing to inspire and delight.

Make It Your Own.

Vegemite isn’t limited to being a toast topping. Its distinctive, savory character opens the door to a wealth of culinary creativity. Discover how its rich flavor can enhance your recipes and inspire you to experiment in the kitchen.
